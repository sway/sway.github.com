A fresh start

posted on 01-01-2012 by Honza

Farewell to 2011, welcome to 2012. One of the greatest years of my life has ended, so it’s time to clear the backlog and start again.

The last two weeks in Korea were quite demanding. I was so smart to leave all the stuff from the second quarter for the last moment, so I had to deal with three exams, two reports and a presentation in the last 10 days. No fun.

Apart from school, however, it was quite nice. We had a closing dinner and party night with fellow exchange students, went for a dinner with Jin and Kayla, me and Marc did some souvenir shopping, I finally went for the ice cream with Kate, sent a package back home (well, to Holland) and managed to stuff all my stuff into the suitcase (26kg).

The flight home was long and boring, except for one nice moment when I met one of the Czech girls from KDI in Dubai (she had seat 30G) and one friend of a friend coming from Angola (31F). And yes, I had 31G. So, on a Friday afternoon, after spending the last 27 hours on the way, crossing 8 timezones, eating 4 airplane meals, and watching 2 movies1, our Airbus A330-200 landed in Prague and I was back home.

This blog is something like my zillionth attempt to write a one, because I am not very good at keeping a regularly updated blog running. However, because I managed to regularly update Seoul Exchange, the blog about my Korean adventures, I decided to give it a shot with my regular life as well. So, stay tuned!

Btw. I will move the posts from there here as well, but it will take some time since the WordPress markup is somehow f-ed up and difficult to convert automagically.
