A fresh start

posted on 01-01-2012 by Honza

Farewell to 2011, welcome to 2012. One of the greatest years of my life has ended, so it’s time to clear the backlog and start again.

The last two weeks in Korea were quite demanding. I was so smart to leave all the stuff from the second quarter for the last moment, so I had to deal with three exams, two reports and a presentation in the last 10 days. No fun.

Fortunately, everything worked out well, and even though I don’t know my grades yet, I hope it’s gonna be OK…

Apart from school stuff, the last few days in Seoul were lovely. On Tuesday we had the last group dinner (bulgogi and lots of soju) and then went to Hongdae for the last time. On Wednesday, apart from being hungover and finishing one report, we went for a dinner with the girls from the International Office, and even though we originally planned to get something near the campus, we ended up going to Apujeong to a nice thai place. After coming home at 12:30am I finished my KBC report and went to to print it out and hand it in. Seriously, going to the lab at 3am felt so Korean. Eventually, I got to bed at 5:30-ish after packing my suitcase and saying goodbye to André and Karin that left the dorm at 4:30.

On Thursday I went for an ice cream with Kate, finished packing and waited for 7:30pm to leave the dorm, head to the international office to check out and then take a taxi and the train to Incheon. Saying goodbye to the girls in the international office was sad, because they were really awesome to us during the 4 months.
